Ronaldo departs from united

Durden faye November 22, 2022
Source: new york post

Days after giving an explosive interview slamming manager Erik ten Hag and the club's owners, Cristiano Ronaldo has announced that he would quit Manchester United "with immediate effect."

“Following conversations with Manchester United we have mutually agreed to end our contract early,” Ronaldo said. “I love Manchester United and I love the fans, that will never ever change. However, it feels like the right time for me to seek a new challenge.

Ronaldo, who is currently in Qatar with Portugal attempting to win his country's first World Cup title, has been left frustrated after being reduced to a fringe member of United's team this season.
